About Us

Shubh Fashion is an export aggregator based in Kolkata dealing specifically in air cargo shipments. We procure fabric and all kinds of textiles (especially women's ethnic wear) from suppliers in Kolkata and other cities (Surat, Delhi, Ahmedabad), and export them to Bangladesh (the biggest importer of Indian ethnic wear).
